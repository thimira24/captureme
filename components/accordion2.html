How can i record my screen with CaptureMe?

Step 1:   Enable your audio settings, Microphone, and Computer Sound based on your requirement
Step 2:   Click on the get started- it's free button
Step 3:   Select your screen and enable "share system sound" checkbox on popup screen
Step 4:   Click on Start recording
Step 5:   Click on stop recording when you are done and download.

Is CaptureMe supporting mobiles?

Currently, CaptureMe does not support mobile devices and, the Safari web browser due
to technical limitations. Try Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Opera for
seamless screen recording.

Is CaptureMe secure to use?

Yes, Absolutely! CaptureMe is secure to use. No one can access your screen recordings
or your computer.
